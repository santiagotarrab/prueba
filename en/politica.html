Quality and environmental policy

Bruerre Eme SA as a company to the service of its customers and the whole community of which it takes part, and in order to give priority to the quality of the products and services that provides, and to contribute to the preservation of the environment, has as main aims:

  • To satisfy the need s and expectations of its interested parties and to gain participation in the markets.
  • To protect sustainable resources, establishing environmental protection guidelines in its industrial speciality.

For the announced, the company establishes the following actions:

  • To establish the aims, quality and environmental goals and carry out their periodical review.
  • To deliver quality products, adjusted to the requirements specified by the company or customers.
  • To fulfil the laws, regulations and procedures related to the products, environmental protection and other requirements that the company supports.
  • To continuously improve quality and environmental strategies of product manufacture.
  • To prevent pollution and minimize negative environmental impacts on natural resources, mainly water.
  • To train its personnel, so as to be aware of environmental care and quality issues.
  • To carry out processes so as to minimize risks for products, people or environment.
  • To regularly evaluate the convenience, suitability, effectiveness and continuous alignment of the quality and environmental management system with the strategies of the company, by means of the provided tools for it.

The President assures that quality and environmental policy is communicated internally, to the interested parties, it is understood and applied by the whole company personnel and the necessary and sufficient resources are assigned according to the established requirements.

ENVIRONMENTAL PERFORMANCE

Bruerre Eme S.A. has implemented and maintains an Integrated Management System (Quality and Environment) which is certified according to the ISO 9001 and ISO 14001 standards since 1997 and 1999, respectively.

The company has an industrial establishment located in Province of Buenos Aires, maintaining an afforestation program of the property and having achieved an index of 150 trees per hectare. In this way it contributes to mitigate the effects of climate change.

On the other hand, as a way to improve the environmental performance, the company has a policy of continuous reduction of the kilograms of hazardous waste generated per metric ton of processed product.
